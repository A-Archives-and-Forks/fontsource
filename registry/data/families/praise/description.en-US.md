Praise is a versatile script with variations from Casual (non-connecting) to Formal appeal.

With nearly 3400 glyphs, the five stylistic sets gives a powerful solution to the design needs of the graphic design professional.

It comes with Latin Character sets including Western, Central, and Vietnamese language support.

To contribute, see [github.com/googlefonts/praise-script](https://github.com/googlefonts/praise-script).
