"Yuji" is a series of fonts digitizing handwriting by the calligrapher Yuji Kataoka. Yuji Mai expresses a free, unrestrained, emotional beauty. The Latin alphabets were written to match the kana strokes.

Fonts in the Yuji Family:

-   [Yuji Boku](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Yuji+Boku)
-   [Yuji Syuku](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Yuji+Syuku)

To contribute to the project, visit [github.com/Kinutafontfactory/Yuji](https://github.com/Kinutafontfactory/Yuji)
