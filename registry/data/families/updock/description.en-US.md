What's Updock? Other than a famous query by an even more famous bunny, it's a beautiful script font with a nearly upright stress.

Updock is an extremely legible formal script with clean connectors and a variety of of discretionary ligatures.

It comes with Latin Character sets including Western, Central, and Vietnamese language support.

To contribute, see [github.com/googlefonts/updock](https://github.com/googlefonts/updock).
