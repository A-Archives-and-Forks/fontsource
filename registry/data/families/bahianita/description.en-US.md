Bahianita is an evolution of the [Bahiana](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Bahiana) project, adding lowercase. It has a rustic, fresh and casual appearance, which has been influenced by wood carving. Its proportions are ideal for composing condensed titles and short texts.

To contribute, see [github.com/Omnibus-Type/Bahiana/tree/master/Bahianita](https://github.com/Omnibus-Type/Bahiana/tree/master/Bahianita)
