Noto Serif Old Uyghur is a design for the historical Central Asian _Old Uyghur_ script.

Noto Serif Old Uyghur contains 132 glyphs, 9 OpenType features, and supports 34 characters from the Unicode block Old Uyghur.

### Supported writing systems

#### Old Uyghur

Old Uyghur is a historical Central Asian abjad. Was used in Turfanand Gansu in c. 700s–1800s for the Old Uyghur language, a variety of Old Turkic. Evolved into the Mongolian and Manchu scripts. Read more on [ScriptSource](https://scriptsource.org/scr/Ougr), [Unicode](https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode15.0.0/ch14.pdf#G58161), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_15924:Ougr), [Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Old_Uyghur_script), [r12a](https://r12a.github.io/scripts/links?iso=Ougr).
