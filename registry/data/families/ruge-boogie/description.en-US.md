Smack a piñata or celebrate Cinco de Mayo with Ruge Boogie. This two-font set is a fun and bouncy style with a joyful flair. Great for scrapbooking, tubes, and other fun stuff.

It comes with Latin Character sets including Western, Central, and Vietnamese language support.

To contribute, see [github.com/googlefonts/ruge-boogie](https://github.com/googlefonts/ruge-boogie).
