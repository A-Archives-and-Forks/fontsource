Funnel Sans and [](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Funnel+Sans)Funnel Display are modern sans-serif typefaces with both clarity and character, originally developed by NORD ID and Kristian Möller for Funnel. The typefaces are inspired by the movement and shapes of data points.

Funnel Sans is a functional yet personal sans-serif, featuring both square and circular shapes in its letterforms.

To contribute, see [github.com/Dicotype/Funnel](https://github.com/Dicotype/Funnel).
