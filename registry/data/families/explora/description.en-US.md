Explora is a beautiful calligraphic typeface with swash forms. Its light and delicate strokes contribute to its elegance. In addition, it features one of the few fonts that contains the entire Cherokee Nation language glyphs.

It comes with Latin Character sets including Western, Central, and Vietnamese language support.

To contribute, see [github.com/googlefonts/explora](https://github.com/googlefonts/explora).
