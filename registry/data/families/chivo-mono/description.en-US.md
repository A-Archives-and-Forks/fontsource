Chivo (‘goat’ in Spanish) is the first Omnibus-Type neo-grotesque typeface family. Its solidness and balanced strokes give Chivo both elegance and practicality.

Chivo Mono is the monospace sibling of [Chivo](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Chivo). Likewise, Chivo Mono is a variable font ranging from Thin to Black with matching Italics.

The family was developed by Héctor Gatti. To contribute, see [github.com/Omnibus-Type/Chivo](https://github.com/Omnibus-Type/Chivo).
