Agdasima is a DocRepair font, intended to improve compliance with the ISO/IEC 29500 standard by providing fallback for Agency FB that minimizes text reflow in Office Open XML documents. Agdasima is based on Big Shoulders, a condensed American Gothic sans-serif font family.

To contribute, please visit [github.com/docrepair-fonts/agdasima-fonts](https://github.com/docrepair-fonts/agdasima-fonts).
