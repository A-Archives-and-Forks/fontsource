An ornate version of Sansita, with curvy uppercase letters, certain alternative lowercase letters and new ligatures.

Sansita Swashed is designed by Pablo Cosgaya (Omnibus-Type) and developed by Aldo De Losa. To contribute, please see [github.com/Omnibus-Type/Sansita-Swashed](https://github.com/Omnibus-Type/Sansita-Swashed).
