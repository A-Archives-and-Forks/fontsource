Geist Pixel is a display typeface family of five pixel-based styles, each with a distinct visual character. It joins Vercel's Geist family alongside its sans-serif and monospace counterparts, [Geist](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Geist) and [Geist Mono](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Geist+Mono).

The five styles — Square, Circle, Grid, Triangle, and Line — each build letters from a different pixel element, giving every variant its own look. Geist Pixel is made for decorative use in headlines, logos, and other display contexts where a pixelated aesthetic is desired.

Created by Vercel, Geist embodies their design principles of simplicity, minimalism, and speed, drawing inspiration from the renowned Swiss design movement. To contribute, see [github.com/vercel/geist-font](https://github.com/vercel/geist-font).
