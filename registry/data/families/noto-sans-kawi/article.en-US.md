Noto Sans Kawi is a design for the historical Southeast Asian _Kawi_ script.

Noto Sans Kawi has multiple weights, contains 110 glyphs, 2 OpenType features, and supports 88 characters from the Unicode block Kawi.

### Supported writing systems

#### Kawi

Kawi is a historical Southeast Asian abugida, written left-to-right. Read more on [ScriptSource](https://scriptsource.org/scr/Kawi), [Unicode](https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode15.0.0/ch17.pdf#G40419), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_15924:Kawi), [r12a](https://r12a.github.io/scripts/links?iso=Kawi).
