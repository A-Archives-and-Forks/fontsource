[Ribeye](http://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Ribeye) and [Ribeye Marrow](http://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Ribeye+Marrow) are reminiscent of a cartoon tattoo style of lettering, but exhibit a playfulness that breaks traditional weight distribution across its letterforms.

An edgy attitude, friendly syncopation, and highly legible letterforms makes these fonts a real pair of charmers.
