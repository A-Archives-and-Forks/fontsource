Corinthia flows with perfect connections and beautiful curves. It’s a delightful design that offers wide usage...

All three weights are perfect for creating elegant design work from packaging and romance novels, to invitations and social expression products.

This award winning font comes with over 500 glyphs, covering Latin Western, Central, and Vietnamese language support.

To contribute, see [github.com/googlefonts/corinthia](https://github.com/googlefonts/corinthia).
