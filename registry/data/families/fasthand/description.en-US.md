Fasthand is a Khmer font for body text. The Khmer design is inspired by a popular style of Khmer handwriting letterforms that are written quickly. The Latin design is a copy of [Seaweed Script](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Seaweed+Script), by Neapolitan.

To contribute, see [github.com/danhhong/Fasthand](https://github.com/danhhong/Fasthand)
