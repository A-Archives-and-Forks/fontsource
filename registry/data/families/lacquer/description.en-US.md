Lacquer is an expressive display font featuring heavy drips and dozens of alternate glyphs. Lacquer was hand drawn using a paint pen by [Niki Polyocan](https://www.nikipolyocan.com/) and was extrapolated and finished by [Eli Block](https://eli-block.com/) at Google Creative Lab. You can see all of Lacquer’s glyphs on the font’s [web specimen](http://lacquer.eli-block.com/).

To contribute, see [github.com/Lacquer-Font/Lacquer](https://github.com/Lacquer-Font/Lacquer).
