Hanalei Fill is for the Polynesian fan. Inspired by the bamboo lettering of the iconic Mai Kai restaurant logo, Hanalei Fill has all the flavor of the genre without compromise. Great for titling and larger typesetting. Hanalei Fill is a compliment to [Hanalei](http://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Hanalei) (the outline version.)

Designed by Brian J. Bonislawsky for Astigmatic (AOETI). To contribute to the project contact Brian J. Bonislawsky.
