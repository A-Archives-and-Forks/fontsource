BioRhyme is a Latin typeface family comprised of two widths, a [normal](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/BioRhyme) family and an expanded family. Each family has 5 weights, and both are intended for use in large and medium sizes.

To contribute, see [github.com/aoifemooney/makingbiorhyme](https://github.com/aoifemooney/makingbiorhyme)
