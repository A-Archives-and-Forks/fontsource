Qahiri is a [Kufic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kufic) typeface based on the modernized and regularized old manuscript Kufic calligraphy style of the late master of Arabic calligraphy, [Mohammad Abdul Qadir](https://ar.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D9%85%D8%AD%D9%85%D8%AF_%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%AF_%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%82%D8%A7%D8%AF%D8%B1_%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%AF_%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%84%D9%87_(%D8%AE%D8%B7%D8%A7%D8%B7)).

Following the convention of naming Kufic styles after the cities they appeared in, Qahiri (قاهري) is named after the city of Cairo, Egypt (القاهرة).
