Khula (खुला) is a contemporary text Devanagari typeface family designed by Erin McLaughlin as a compliment to [Open Sans](http://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Open+Sans), a Latin family designed by Steve Matteson. Currently it has 5 weights and supports Hindi.
