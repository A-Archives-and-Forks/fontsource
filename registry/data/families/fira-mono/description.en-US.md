Designed to integrate with the character of the FirefoxOS, the Fira typefaces also aim to cover the legibility needs for a large range of handsets varying in screen quality and rendering.

The Fira font family comes in a [Sans Serif](http://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Fira+Sans) with 4 weights (Light, Regular, Medium and Bold) all accompanied by italic styles. The package also includes this Mono Spaced variant with 3 weights (Regular, Medium, Bold.)

See the [Mozilla FirefoxOS Style Guide](http://www.mozilla.org/en-US/styleguide/products/firefox-os/typeface/) for more details.
