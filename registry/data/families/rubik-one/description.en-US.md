Rubik is a sans serif font family with slightly rounded corners designed by Philipp Hubert and Sebastian Fischer at [Hubert & Fischer](http://hubertfischer.com/) as part of the [Chrome Cube Lab](https://www.chrome.com/cubelab) project.

Rubik One is the initial version of the Black roman style in the [Rubik](https://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Rubik) family, which has 5 weights. It is paired with [Rubik Mono One](https://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Rubik+Mono+One), a monospaced variation of the design.
