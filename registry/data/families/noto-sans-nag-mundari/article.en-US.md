Noto Sans Nag Mundari is a design for the Indic _Nag Mundari_ script.

Noto Sans Nag Mundari has multiple weights, contains 66 glyphs, and supports 63 characters from 2 Unicode blocks: Nag Mundari, Basic Latin.

### Supported writing systems

#### Nag Mundari

Nag Mundari is an Indic alphabet, written left-to-right. Read more on [ScriptSource](https://scriptsource.org/scr/Nagm), [Unicode](https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode15.0.0/ch15.pdf#G112439), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_15924:Nagm), [r12a](https://r12a.github.io/scripts/links?iso=Nagm).
