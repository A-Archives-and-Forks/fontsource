Noto Serif Vithkuqi is a design for the historical European _Vithkuqi_ script.

Noto Serif Vithkuqi has multiple weights, contains 103 glyphs, and supports 101 characters from 2 Unicode blocks: Vithkuqi, Basic Latin.

### Supported writing systems

#### Vithkuqi

Vithkuqi (Büthakukye) is a historical European bicameral alphabet, written left-to-right. Created around 1840 by Naum Veqilharxhi for the Albanian language. Read more on [ScriptSource](https://scriptsource.org/scr/Vith), [Unicode](https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode15.0.0/ch08.pdf#G40863), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_15924:Vith), [r12a](https://r12a.github.io/scripts/links?iso=Vith).
