The idea of this typeface is to be geometric, elegant, with a vintage feeling, for use at larger sizes. It is inspired by geometric sans serif designs from the 1920s. The x-height is half way from baseline to cap height, an unusual proportion.

There is a sister family, [Josefin Slab](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Josefin+Slab)

In December 2019, it was updated with a Variable Font "Weight" axis.

To contribute, see [github.com/googlefonts/josefinsans](https://github.com/googlefonts/josefinsans).
