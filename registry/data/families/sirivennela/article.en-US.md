Sirivennela is a script-style Telugu Unicode font. It is mainly intended for headings and subheadings, but can also be used for text at large point sizes.

This font was developed and named after the late Sirivennela Seetharama Shastry, a renowned lyricist in the Telugu film industry. To contribute, please see [github.com/appajid/Sirivennela](https://github.com/appajid/Sirivennela).
