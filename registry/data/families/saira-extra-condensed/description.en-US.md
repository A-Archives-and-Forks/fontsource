Saira is a sans serif system. It features a huge range of weights and widths, ready for all kind of typographic challenges.

This is the Extra Condensed family, which is part of the superfamily along with [Normal](http://fonts.google.com/specimen/Saira), [Semi Condensed](http://fonts.google.com/specimen/Saira+Semi+Condensed), and [Condensed](http://fonts.google.com/specimen/Saira+Condensed), each with 9 weights.

This typeface was designed by Hector Gatti and developed by the Omnibus Type team. To contribute to the project, visit [github.com/Omnibus-Type](https://github.com/Omnibus-Type)
