Bubbler One is an original font with thin strokes that are particularly straight. It is a legible typeface in small sizes. You can use Bubbler One for any kind of text, formal or informal, big or small; I hope you find it useful!

Designed by [Brenda Gallo](https://plus.google.com/u/0/103824723792416046218/about) and [Gustavo Dipre](https://plus.google.com/u/0/111231144350963303515/about).
