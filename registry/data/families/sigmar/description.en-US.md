Sigmar is a casual, display specific webfont. It's design is based upon various fonts found in mid twentieth century pulp magazine advertising. It's an improved version of [Sigmar One](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Sigmar+One), whose small caps have been replaced by basic lower cases, for the Summer of Type in 2023.

To contribute, see [github.com/googlefonts/sigmarone](https://github.com/googlefonts/sigmarone).
