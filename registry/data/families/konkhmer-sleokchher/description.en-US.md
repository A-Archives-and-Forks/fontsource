Konkhmer Sleokchher is created and released in 2015 by Suon May Sophanith. It is a modern display Khmer font, inspired by the brush strokes used for writing on walls, but expressed as leaves. With a medium size, it is ideal for use as headings or in large typography.

To contribute, see [github.com/suonmaysophanith7/KonKhmer\_SleokChher](https://github.com/suonmaysophanith7/KonKhmer_SleokChher).
