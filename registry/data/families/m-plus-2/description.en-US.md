M+ FONTS is a little nifty font family for everyday usage. Mplus 2 is a Sans Serif font with nine weights from Thin to Black, supporting 5,700+ Kanjis for Japanese with GF Latin Plus. With the somewhat classic letterforms, this font pursues new standard of classic modern.

To contribute to the project, visit [github.com/coz-m/MPLUS\_FONTS](https://github.com/coz-m/MPLUS_FONTS)
