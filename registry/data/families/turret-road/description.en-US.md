Turret Road is a geometric sans serif typeface, with very low contrast, high x-height, high glyph height, and playful diacritics.

Turret Road delves into eugenics, solar evolution, science fiction and homeward conversations. For more information, see its [project overview](https://github.com/noponies/Turret-Road/blob/master/documentation/project-overview.md).

To contribute, see [github.com/noponies/Turret-Road](https://github.com/noponies/Turret-Road).
