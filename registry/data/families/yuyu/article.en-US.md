Yuyu is a slender, stylized handwritten typeface designed by Julia García López. Originally created as a personal tool for the designer's own visual identity, Yuyu is the product of years of manual refinement and care.

Its tall, uppercase letterforms carry a pronounced verticality that feels both fun and familiar, while resolving into a mature and cheerful finish. This balance makes Yuyu a natural fit for personal branding, editorial design, modern titling, and any project looking to build a friendly, human connection.

The family consists of two variations that share the same capital letters: Yuyu Mid and Yuyu Short. Yuyu Mid was developed with a clear focus on legibility and accessibility, making it the more practical choice for extended use. Yuyu Short, by contrast, is intended purely for aesthetic and display purposes.

_"This font is, in many ways, like a child to me. It has featured in every sketch and project for years. Seeing it today transformed into a complete typographic tool is the start of a new chapter. This is my voice, now available for yours."_ — Julia García López
