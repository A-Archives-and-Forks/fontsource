Caladea is a free modern, friendly serif font family based on [Cambo](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cambo), designed by Carolina Giovagnoli and Andrés Torresi for HT Fonts.

Caladea has the following differences:

-   More condensed width
-   New metrics
-   4 styles. Regular, Bold, Italic, and Bold Italic
-   Character set expanded to WGL (418 glyphs)

To contribute, see [github.com/huertatipografica/Caladea](https://github.com/huertatipografica/Caladea).
