Based on [Libre Franklin](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Libre+Franklin), Public Sans is a strong, neutral typeface for interfaces, text, and headings. It was Developed by the [United States Web Design System](https://v2.designsystem.digital.gov/).

The family was upgraded to a variable font in May 2022.

To contribute, see [github.com/uswds/public-sans](https://github.com/uswds/public-sans)
