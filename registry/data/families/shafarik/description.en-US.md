The Shafarik font, named after [Pavel Jozef Šafárik](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pavel_Jozef_%C5%A0af%C3%A1rik) (1795–1861), Slovak-born scholar and one of the founders of modern Slavic philology, is a specialized font intended for an academic presentation of Old Church Slavonic (OCS) texts written in either the Cyrillic or Glagolitic alphabets.

To contribute, please see [github.com/slavonic/Shafarik](https://github.com/slavonic/Shafarik).
