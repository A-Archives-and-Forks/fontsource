The Content fonts are designed for readable and beautiful rendering of text in the Khmer script, the national language of Cambodia. The designer, [Danh Hong](http://www.khmertype.blogspot.com/), has many years of experience creating fonts for Khmer and other Southeast Asian languages, and is actively involved in the [KhmerOS](http://www.khmertype.blogspot.com/) project, dedicated to a vision where Cambodians can learn and use computers in their own language.
