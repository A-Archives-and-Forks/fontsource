MuseoModerno is a contemporary geometric typeface for the new Identity of the Museum of Modern Art of Buenos Aires (Museo Moderno, AR). Designed by Marcela Romero, Héctor Gatti, Pablo Cosgaya and the Omnibus-Type Team.

The June 2022 release completes the family with the italic.

To contribute, see [github.com/Omnibus-Type/MuseoModerno](https://github.com/Omnibus-Type/MuseoModerno).
