Palanquin is a Unicode-compliant Latin and Devanagari text type family designed for the digital age. The Devanagari is monolinear and was designed alongside the sans serif Latin.

It currently comprises of seven text weights, and is extended with a heavier display family, [Palanquin Dark](http://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Palanquin+Dark), that has many subtle design details to accommodate the darker typographic color. The Palanquin superfamily is versatile and strikes a balance between typographic conventions and that bit of sparkle.
