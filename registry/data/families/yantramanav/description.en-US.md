Yantramanav (यंत्रमानव) is a Devanagari typeface family designed by Erin McLaughlin.

The style and weights of Yantramanav's Devanagari were designed as a compliment to [Roboto](http://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Roboto), a Latin family designed by Christian Robertson.

This project is led by Erin McLaughlin, an independent typeface designer, font developer, and consultant who specializes in Indic fonts. To contribute, see [github.com/erinmclaughlin/Yantramanav](https://github.com/erinmclaughlin/Yantramanav)
