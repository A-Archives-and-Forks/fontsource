Belanosima is a DocRepair font, intended to improve compliance with the ISO/IEC 29500 standard by providing fallback for Berlin Sans FB that minimizes text reflow in Office Open XML documents. Belanosima is based on Josefin Sans, a geometric, elegant family with a vintage feeling, for use at larger sizes. Josefin Sans is inspired by geometric sans serif designs from the 1920s.

To contribute, please visit [github.com/docrepair-fonts/belanosima-fonts](https://github.com/docrepair-fonts/belanosima-fonts).
