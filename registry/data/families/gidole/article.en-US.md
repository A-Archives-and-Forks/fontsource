Gidole is a small, beautiful mountain town in southern Ethiopia where the font’s author grew up. The font, his first, is a humanist and minimal variation of the original [DIN 1451](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DIN_1451) design.

The author wishes that you thank him for the design by donating to the [Ethiopian Red Cross Society](https://redcrosseth.org/).

To contribute, see https://github.com/googlefonts/gidole.
